## Welcome to Gale Payments

Gale Payments simplifies the process of accepting Health Savings Account (HSA) and Flexible Spending Account (FSA) payments. Our platform allows merchants to integrate HSA/FSA payment options into their WooCommerce and Shopify stores, providing a seamless checkout experience while ensuring compliance with IRS regulations.

## Why Choose Gale Payments?

Our platform is designed to provide value to merchants and customers alike. By using Gale Payments, you can offer your customers a convenient way to pay with their HSA/FSA cards while ensuring all transactions are compliant with industry standards.
