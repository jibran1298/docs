This guide will walk you through the process of installing and configuring Gale Payments on your Shopify store, enabling HSA/FSA payments for your customers.

---

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure the following:
- **Shopify Admin Access**: You have administrative access to your Shopify store.
- **Gale Payments API Key**: You received your API Key during the Gale Payments onboarding process.

---

## Installation Steps



<Steps>
  <Step title="Install the Gale Payments App">
    1. Install the [ Gale HSA & FSA Payments App ](https://apps.shopify.com/gale-hsa-fsa-payments) 
    2. Follow the prompts to add the payments app to your store.

    <img
      className="block"
      src="/images/shopify_install.png"
      alt="Install Gale Payments App"
    />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Store Details and API Key">
    1. After installing the app, you will be redirected to Configure Gale Payments App
    2. Enter your **Store Name** and **API Key** provided during onboarding.

    <img
      className="block"
      src="/images/shopify_configure.png"
      alt="Install Gale Payments App"
    />

    3. **Save** your settings.

     <img
      className="block"
      src="/images/shopify_return.png"
      alt="Install Gale Payments App"
    />

    4. Click **Return to Shopify**.

  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure Gale Payments in Shopify">
    1. Go to **Shopify Admin > Settings > Payments**.
    2. Click **Add Payment Method**.
    3. Select **Search by Provider** and type **"Gale"**.

     <img
      className="block"
      src="/images/shopify_search.png"
      alt="Install Gale Payments App"
    />

    4. Choose **Gale HSA & FSA Payments** from the list.

     <img
      className="block"
      src="/images/shopify_activate.png"
      alt="Install Gale Payments App"
    />


    5. Enable **Test Mode** and click **Activate**.

   
  </Step>

  <Step title="Setup Gale  Pixel ">
    1. Go to **Shopify Admin > Settings > Customer Events**.
    2. Click **Add Custom Pixel**.
    3. Enter Pixel Name **"Gale Pixel"**.

     <img
      className="block"
      src="/images/shopify_add_pixel.png"
      alt="Add Gale Pixel "
    />

    4. Enter **Pixel Code** Provided during Onboarding and click **Connect** to save.

     <img
      className="block"
      src="/images/shopify_pixel_code.png"
      alt="Add Gale Pixel Code"
    />

 <img
      className="block"
      src="/images/shopify_pixel_status.png"
      alt="Add Gale Pixel Code"
    />
  
    5. Make sure the Pixel status is **Connected**. 

    




  </Step>

  <Step title="Test the Integration">
    1. Go to your store and add a product to the cart.
    2. Proceed to checkout and select **Gale HSA & FSA Payments** as the payment method.

     <img
      className="block"
      src="/images/shopify_checkout.png"
      alt="Gale Checkout "
    />


    3. Use one of the [Test Cards](/testcards) for the transaction.

 <img
      className="block"
      src="/images/shopify_journey.png"
      alt="Gale Checkout Journey"
    />

    4. Verify the **order** and **payment status** in your Shopify Admin to ensure that test transaction is successful.

    
  </Step>

  <Step title="Go Live">
    1. Return to the Gale Payments app in your Shopify Admin.
    2. Disable **Test Mode** in the settings.

     <img
      className="block"
      src="/images/shopify_production.png"
      alt="Disable Test Mode "
    />

    3. **Save** your changes and you are ready to accept **HSA & FSA** cards using Gale Payments.

   
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

- **App Not Visible**: Ensure the Gale Payments app is installed and activated in your Shopify Admin.
- **API Key Issues**: Double-check your API Key for accuracy.
- **Test Transactions Failing**: Confirm you are using valid [ Test Cards](/testcards).
